Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026  ·  UPSC PYQ by Ufaq

1. Introduction

UPSC PYQ ("we", "our", or "us") is operated by Ufaq. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information
When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. When you sign in with email OTP, we collect your email address.

Usage Data
We collect data about your activity in the app including questions attempted, answers given, accuracy scores, bookmarks, and study streaks. This data is used solely to provide your performance dashboard and study analytics.

Device Information
We may collect basic device information such as device type and operating system version for technical support and app improvement purposes.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To track and display your study progress and performance
  • To maintain your bookmarks and streaks
  • To improve app functionality and user experience
  • To send OTP emails for authentication

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Sign-In (Google LLC) — for authentication
  • Google Analytics for Firebase — for app analytics

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your data.

6. Data Retention

We retain your account and usage data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your account at any time from the Profile screen, which will permanently delete all your data from our servers.

7. Children's Privacy

UPSC PYQ is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, delete your account from the app or contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
