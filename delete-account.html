Delete Your Account

UPSC PYQ by Ufaq

You can permanently delete your UPSC PYQ account and all associated data directly from within the app.

How to Delete Your Account In-App

  1. Open the UPSC PYQ app
  2. Tap the Profile tab at the bottom
  3. Scroll down to the bottom of the page
  4. Tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm the deletion when prompted
Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone.

What Data Gets Deleted

  • Your account and profile information
  • All question attempt history and accuracy stats
  • All bookmarked questions
  • Your study streaks and performance data

Data Retention

All personal data is permanently deleted from our servers immediately upon account deletion. No data is retained after deletion.

Need Help?

If you are unable to access the app to delete your account, contact us and we will delete your account manually within 7 days.
